## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how TreSapo ("App") handles user information. Please review and agree to this Privacy Policy before using the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

The App does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, postal address, or phone number.

However, for quality improvement, bug fixes, service stability, and feature enhancement, we may collect the following anonymous information, which does not identify individuals:

- Crash reports (information when the app unexpectedly terminates)
- Statistical usage data (such as frequency of use for specific features)
- Device information (such as OS version and device model)

Such information may be automatically collected through frameworks and related services such as Expo.

## 3. Purpose of Use

Collected anonymous information is used for the following purposes:

- Bug fixes and stability improvement of the App
- Service improvement and feature development
- Usage analysis

## 4. Provision to Third Parties

We do not provide collected information to third parties, except when required by law or with the user's consent.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy as needed. The revised policy becomes effective upon notification in the App or by another appropriate method at the time of publication.
